Frequently Asked Questions

What is included with membership?

Membership includes access to our world-class amenities including golf, spa, dining, fitness, pool, and a curated calendar of member events. Specific inclusions vary by membership tier.

How do I apply for membership?

Membership is by invitation and inquiry. Contact our membership team at (307) 249-1000 or submit an inquiry through this site. A member of our team will guide you through the process.

Can I visit before becoming a member?

Yes. We offer curated discovery visits for prospective members. These include a tour of the property, amenities, and accommodations, along with a complimentary dining experience.

Are memberships transferable?

Founding and Legacy memberships include transfer rights. Full and Social memberships may be transferable under certain conditions. Please contact our team for specific details.

What is the guest policy?

Members may bring guests to enjoy the club's amenities. Guest policies and complimentary guest passes vary by membership tier. Our concierge team can provide full details.
