Discover Us

Our Story

Born from a reverence for the untamed beauty of the Teton Valley, the Hoback Club was founded in 2024 with a singular vision: to create a sanctuary where refined living meets the grandeur of the American West. Nestled at the base of the Jackson Hole Mountain Resort, our 2,900-acre retreat offers an unparalleled connection to nature, community, and the extraordinary.

The Setting

Jackson Hole & The Grand Tetons

Set against the dramatic backdrop of the Teton Range — where peaks rise to nearly 14,000 feet — the Hoback Club occupies one of the most spectacular settings in North America. The Snake River winds through the valley below, offering world-class fly fishing, while the surrounding wilderness provides year-round adventure for those who seek it.
