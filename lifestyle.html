Winter Activities

Winter in Jackson Hole is more than a season -- It's a way of life. From the first snowfall to the last run of spring, the valley transforms into a world-class winter playground, framed by the iconic peaks of the Tetons and the quiet luxury of mountain living.

Step out your door into pristine powder and legendary terrain, with ski-in/ski-out access to Jackson Hole Mountain Resort. Beyond the slopes, discover the rhythm of winter at your own pace -- snowshoe through silent forests, ride a horse-drawn sleigh, or witness wildlife in the serenity of snow-covered landscapes.

Let our Club Concierge take care of every detail -- every activity below can be arranged for you.

Skiing

Ski Passes

As daily ski passes may be limited, we will assist you in lift passes for you and your family.

Ski Lessons

If you require full or half day lessons, please reach out to our Club Concierge.

Private Ski Fitting

Access private demo fleet and premium gear, all in the comfort and privacy of your residence. Contact our Club Concierge to reserve your personal fitting.

Backcountry Ski Guiding

Takes you beyond the resort gates into untouched terrain, with small-group adventures with lift access coordinated at booking to ensure a seamless experience.

Heli Skiing

Embark on the ultimate heli-skiing or snowboarding adventure in Jackson Hole, with small groups, epic terrain, and a full day of powder including lunch.

Cross-Country / Nordic Skiing

Explore over 14 miles of groomed Nordic trails in Grand Teton National Park with expert-led or self-guided cross-country ski tours, perfect for all levels and available in half- or full-day experiences with lunch included.

Other Fun Winter Activities

Snowmobiling

Ignite the thrill of snowmobiling with guided or self-guided tours through Yellowstone, Grand Teton, or the Gros Ventre, with all gear, transport, and routes tailored to any skill level.

Sleigh Rides

Ride through crisp mountain air in a vintage horse-drawn sleigh, with sunset or twilight rides enhanced by alpenglow skies, starry nights, and optional fireside apres or gourmet hot chocolate.

Wildlife Safaris

Experience the natural beauty and wildlife of Grand Teton National Park on full- or half-day tours timed for optimal sightings during early mornings or golden hour, with lunch included.

Dog Sledding

Experience the thrill of dog mushing through Wyoming's stunning wilderness with hourly one-hour tours from Teton Village, offering couples and families a scenic ride powered by a skilled husky team.

Snowshoeing

Offering a serene alternative to skiing with guided full- or half-day tours, custom routes, all gear provided, and optional wellness or photography experiences arranged by your Club Concierge.

Snowcoach

Explore Yellowstone's iconic Old Faithful and geothermal wonders in a luxurious snowcoach -- an all-ages, full-day adventure with a relaxed, scenic route.

Ice Skating

Enjoy scenic outdoor skating at Teton Village's Warming Hut rink, open daily from 4-9PM with breathtaking mountain views.

Tubing

Experience thrilling toboggan, tubing, and alpine slide rides at Jackson Hole's family-friendly terrain park, open 10AM-4PM tickets are available.

Alpine Climbing

Jackson Hole offers world-class alpine climbing in the Tetons with technical ascents, custom itineraries, and gear provided for individuals and small groups, including multi-day climbs.

Summer Activities

Summer is where beauty meets the Wild West. As the snow melts and wildflowers bloom, Jackson Hole awakens to a summer season filled with sun-drenched days and endless opportunity for adventure. From fly-fishing on the Snake River to horseback rides, mountain biking and peaceful scenic floats, every moment is framed by the towering Tetons.

Here, the pace is yours to set -- where your Club Concierge can arrange the best guides for you and your family. All activities below can be seamlessly coordinated so you and your family can maximize your stay.

Wildlife Safaris

Embark on a wildlife safari through Yellowstone or Grand Teton National Parks with expert naturalist guides, including transportation and gourmet lunches for full-day or prime-time sunrise and sunset tours.

Horseback Riding

Enjoy a private, guided horseback ride through alpine meadows with expert wranglers, available mornings, afternoons, or sunsets for ages 6 and up, with optional gourmet cast-iron cooking.

Whitewater Rafting / Scenic Floats

Whether seeking whitewater thrills or scenic floats, Jackson Hole's Snake River offers daily full- or half-day trips for all ages and skill levels, with all gear -- including wetsuits and splash jackets -- provided.

Fly-Fishing

Enjoy world-class guided fly fishing on the Snake, Green, Salt Rivers, or local spring creeks, with all gear, transport, and expert instruction tailored to your skill level. A valid Wyoming fishing license is required for all participants.

Hiking Jackson Hole Valley

Start your hike from Teton Village or atop the gondola for sweeping mountain views, then venture beyond the crowds to tranquil trails like Phelps Lake or Hidden Falls within the nearby national parks.

Other Fun Summer Activities

Climbing / Via Ferrata

Explore the Tetons with expert guides or take on the thrilling Via Ferrata just steps from your door -- an exhilarating mix of hiking and climbing, with all gear provided for full, half, or multi-day adventures.

Mountain Biking / Ebike Tours

Whether you prefer a leisurely ride or a thrilling adventure, there's a trail for everyone. Enjoy breathtaking scenic views while riding, even on uncrowded trails. Our Club Concierge will arrange for rentals or scenic tours.

ATV / Side-By-Sides Tours

Set out on an ATV or side-by-side adventure through the stunning Gros Ventre wilderness and Curtis Canyon, uncovering hidden gems along the way. Drivers must be 16 with a valid license, passengers 8 or older.

Golf & Tennis

Hit the links and have access to three private golf courses exclusively to the Hoback Club.

Wooden Boat Floats

Unwind on a private late-afternoon Snake River float, complete with on-site, chef-prepared meals and sunset views -- perfect for couples, families, or small groups, with transportation available and advanced booking recommended.

Natural Hot Springs

Journey by car, snowmobile or dog sled as you travel to secluded hot spring oasis. Full or half day excursions are available to fit your schedule for every season.

Flying / Helicopter Tours

Charter a private helicopter or Kodiak flight over Jackson Hole with year-round launch options, custom routes, and optional champagne toasts -- ideal for 3-6 passengers, with advance booking recommended.

Hot Air Balloon Tours

Lift off at first light for a serene 3-hour hot air balloon experience over the Tetons, taking advantage of calm morning winds and ending with a celebratory landing. Transportation available upon request.

Paragliding

Soar above Jackson Hole and the Teton Range on a 20-minute tandem paragliding flight with expert pilots -- no experience needed and all gear included for ages 6 and up.

Stargazing

Enjoy year-round evening stargazing tours with astronomy experts, featuring top equipment, cozy blankets, and gourmet beverages -- book through your Club Concierge for prime celestial events.
