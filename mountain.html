Explore Jackson Hole

Discover key destinations in the valley

1
Grand Teton National Park
Iconic peaks rising to 13,775 feet

Iconic peaks rising to 13,775 feet. World-class mountaineering, hiking, and wildlife viewing in one of America's most dramatic landscapes.

2
Hoback Club
An exclusive mountain retreat

An exclusive mountain retreat nestled at the base of Jackson Hole Mountain Resort. Private ski-in/ski-out access with luxury amenities.

3
Snake River
Blue-ribbon trout fishing

Blue-ribbon trout fishing through pristine canyon waters. Scenic float trips beneath the Tetons with opportunities to spot bald eagles, moose, and otters.

4
Jackson Hole Town
Vibrant mountain town culture

A vibrant mountain town centered around the iconic elk antler arches of Town Square. Fine dining, western galleries, and apres-ski culture.

5
Yellowstone National Park
The world's first national park

The world's first national park, just an hour north. Old Faithful, Grand Prismatic Spring, and the largest concentration of wildlife in the lower 48.
