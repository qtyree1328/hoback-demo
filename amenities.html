Welcome to Elevated Living

Member Dining & Gathering Spaces

Year-round private dining at the Hoback Club offers members signature meals from sunrise to apres and dinner, all served with refined mountain hospitality.

Exquisite cuisine masterly curated by our Michelin Star Chef, Jean-Louis Dumonet.

  • Hand selected Old and New World wine cellar features rare vintages and perfect pairings.
  • The Great Hall Dining Room and Deck, with luxe seating and eight private firepits, offers members an exclusive, fireside setting to unwind, dine, and connect year-round.
  • Our Fireside Lounge atmosphere transforms into Jackson Hole's premier apres-ski destination with handcrafted cocktails and hors d'oeuvres.

Private Dining

Private Residence Dining, exclusively for Hoback Club members, offers in-residence chef service and elegant private dining spaces tailored for everything from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations.

Ski

Premium ski lockers and slopeside valet, private garage parking, and direct tram/gondola access -- seamless mountain living.

Seamless ski-in/ski-out access with dedicated ski valet service at the Hoback Club, where every detail -- from gear prep to boot adjustments -- is designed to elevate your mountain experience from slope to suite.

Spa

Lux Euro Spa -- Enhance your experience with spa treatments from our world-ranked massage artists available on-call for you. Residents and guests may request appointments through our Club Concierge.

Premier Wellness Centre with hot cold hydrotherapy circuit, a Himalayan salt relaxation room, and dedicated treatment suites.

Pools

Indoor and outdoor pools, hot tubs, and a yoga lawn -- a full year-round sanctuary for the body, mind and season.

Golf

Trackman Golf Simulator where you can play virtual rounds on hundreds of famous courses or use detailed analytics for game improvement.

Concierge Services

Personalized Concierge Services -- From bespoke dining reservations to tailored local experiences, our dedicated concierge team is available around the clock to assist with your every need.

Fun for the Family

A children's gaming room and dedicated family spaces, ensuring multi-generational comfort with a sophisticated edge.
