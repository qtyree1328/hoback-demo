Amenities

Championship Golf

Our 18-hole championship course winds through alpine meadows with the Teton Range as its backdrop. Designed to challenge and inspire, the course features dramatic elevation changes, natural water features, and immaculate conditioning from spring through fall. A state-of-the-art practice facility and private instruction from PGA professionals complete the experience.

Spa & Wellness

The Hoback Spa is a sanctuary of restoration. Drawing on the healing traditions of the mountain West, our treatments incorporate locally sourced botanicals, mineral-rich waters, and expert therapists. Facilities include treatment rooms with mountain views, a thermal circuit, meditation garden, and a fully equipped yoga and Pilates studio.

Dining

Three distinctive dining venues await. The Lodge offers refined mountain cuisine with seasonal tasting menus. Mountain Grill provides casual slope-side fare and craft cocktails. Après Bar is the perfect gathering place after a day on the mountain, featuring local microbrews, fireside seating, and live music on weekends.

Fitness Center

A world-class fitness facility featuring the latest cardio and strength equipment, personal training suites, and a dedicated group fitness studio. From high-altitude conditioning to recovery sessions, our certified trainers design programs tailored to the mountain lifestyle.

Pool & Hot Springs

Our outdoor heated infinity pool and natural hot springs sit at the edge of the wilderness, offering panoramic mountain views in every season. Imagine soaking under a canopy of stars while snow falls gently around you — this is the Hoback experience at its finest.

Kids Club

The Hoback Kids Club offers supervised adventures for our youngest members and guests. From nature exploration and arts programs to junior ski instruction and campfire nights, every activity is designed to foster a lifelong love of the outdoors in a safe, nurturing environment.
